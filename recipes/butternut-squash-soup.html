Butternut Squash Soup

Description

"This cozy Butternut Squash Soup with Crispy Lentils is the ultimate fall recipe. Featuring creamy pureed squash and a crunchy surprise topping, you'll want to indulge in bowl after bowl!" -Rainbow Plant Life

Ingredients

  • 1 large butternut squash
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 3/4 cup green lentils
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Prehead the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Cook the lentils. Dry well.
  3. Prep and roast the squash
